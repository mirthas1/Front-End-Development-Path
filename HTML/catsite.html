Picture of the Khao Manee cat

The pure white khao manee (pronounced "cow man-ee") is also known as the diamond eye cat due to their trademark jewel-like eyes, which can be blue, green, gold, or "odd" (two eyes of different colors). The khao manee originated naturally in Thailand centuries ago, and their name means "white gem." This petite cat with silky short hair is friendly and outgoing, so they adore attention and playtime.
